GEMSBOK

The gemsbok, gemsbuck or South African oryx is a large antelope in the genus Oryx. It is native to the arid regions of Southern Africa, such as the Kalahari Desert. Some authorities formerly included the East African oryx as a subspecies. Wikipedia Height: 1,2 m (At Shoulder) Scientific name: Oryx gazella Gestation period: 274 days Encyclopedia of Life Length: 1,9 – 2,4 m Speed: 60 km/h (Maximum, Running) Mass: Male: 220 – 300 kg, Female: 100 – 210 kg
